The Diagnostic Entry Point

The Revenue Audit is where every engagement begins. It identifies where your revenue system is failing and provides a clear roadmap to recovery and growth.

Identify Failures

We diagnose failures across six critical categories to understand exactly where your revenue system is breaking down.

Quantify Impact

We calculate the revenue impact of each failure so you understand exactly how much revenue you're leaving on the table.

Create Roadmap

Six Diagnostic Categories

Click each category to explore the diagnostic questions

Demand Failure ▼

Are people aware of your business and interested in what you offer?

Key Questions:

  • Do you have consistent lead flow?
  • Is your brand recognized in your market?
  • Are your marketing channels effective?
  • Do you understand your target audience?
  • Is your value proposition clear?
Capture Failure ▼

Are you converting interested prospects into qualified leads?

Key Questions:

  • Is your website optimized for conversions?
  • Do you have clear calls-to-action?
  • Are you capturing lead data effectively?
  • What's your conversion rate?
  • Do you have lead qualification criteria?
System Failure ▼

Are your revenue operations running efficiently and predictably?

Key Questions:

  • Are your systems integrated and automated?
  • Do you have visibility into your pipeline?
  • Are processes documented and repeatable?
  • Is there accountability for results?
  • Can you scale without adding headcount?
Capability Failure ▼

Does your team have the skills to execute your revenue strategy?

Key Questions:

  • Does your team understand AI and automation?
  • Are team members trained in their roles?
  • Do you have the right people in place?
  • Is there a culture of continuous learning?
  • Can your team execute at scale?
Continuity Failure ▼

Are you retaining customers and building long-term value?

Key Questions:

  • What's your customer retention rate?
  • Do you have recurring revenue models?
  • Are customers getting ongoing value?
  • Do you have upsell and expansion strategies?
  • What's your customer lifetime value?
Authority Failure ▼

Are you positioned as the trusted authority in your market?

Key Questions:

  • Are you recognized as an expert?
  • Do you publish thought leadership content?
  • Do customers trust your brand?
  • Are you winning against competitors?
  • Do prospects seek you out?
