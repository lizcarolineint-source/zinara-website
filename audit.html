The Diagnostic Foundation

The Revenue Audit is where every engagement begins. It identifies where your revenue system is failing and provides a clear roadmap to recovery and growth.

Identify Failures

We diagnose failures across six critical categories to understand exactly where your revenue system is breaking down.

Quantify Impact

We calculate the revenue impact of each failure so you understand exactly how much revenue you're leaving on the table.

Create Roadmap

Six Diagnostic Categories

Demand Failure

Are people aware of your business and interested in what you offer? We evaluate your market visibility and lead flow consistency.

  • Lead flow consistency analysis
  • Brand recognition assessment
  • Marketing channel effectiveness

Capture Failure

Are you converting interested prospects into qualified leads? We analyze your conversion optimization and data capture processes.

  • Conversion rate optimization audit
  • Lead data capture effectiveness
  • Qualification criteria evaluation

System Failure

Are your revenue operations running efficiently and predictably? We audit your integration, automation, and process documentation.

  • Automation and integration audit
  • Pipeline visibility assessment
  • Process repeatability check

Capability Failure

Does your team have the skills to execute your revenue strategy? We assess AI proficiency and continuous learning culture.

  • Team AI proficiency assessment
  • Training and skill-gap analysis
  • Execution capability evaluation

Continuity Failure

Are you retaining customers and building long-term value? We evaluate retention rates and recurring revenue models.

  • Customer retention analysis
  • LTV and expansion strategy audit
  • Recurring revenue model evaluation

Authority Failure

Are you positioned as the trusted authority in your market? We assess thought leadership and competitive positioning.

  • Market authority assessment
  • Thought leadership content audit
  • Competitive positioning analysis
